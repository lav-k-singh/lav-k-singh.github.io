Welcome to my personal webpage

I am a mathematician working in Functional Analysis and Operator Algebras. I did my PhD from the School of Physical Sciences, Jawaharlal Nehru University, New Delhi. Currently I am serving as a postdoctoral researcher at the Institute of Mathematics, Physics and Mechanics, Ljubljana (Slovenia). In the past I have also served as postdoctoral fellow at IISER-Bhopal and IIT-Gandhinagar.

I am primarily interested in Banach,Topological and Operator algebras. The following keywords identify my objects of interest.

  • \(C^*\)-algebras, Locally \(C^*\)-algebras.
  • Group algebras and Fourier algebras of a locally compact group.
  • Biduals of Banach algebras.
  • Arens regularity/ Topological centers of Bidual algebras.
  • Schatten $p$-class ideals.
  • Projective tensor products of Banach spaces/algebras.
  • Projective and inductive limits of topological algebras.
  • Geometric properties of Banach spaces (Radon Nikodym Property, Weak sequential completeness, Covering index)
